Senior Software Engineer — Backend / Platform

Company: Ceegees Software

About the role:
We are hiring a Senior Software Engineer to join our backend platform team.
You will design, build, and operate distributed services that power our
product. The role involves close collaboration with frontend, infrastructure,
and product teams.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable backend services and REST/GraphQL APIs.
- Improve performance, reliability, and observability of production systems.
- Work with relational and NoSQL databases; design schemas and queries.
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation.
- Mentor junior engineers and participate in code reviews.

Qualifications:
- 4+ years of experience building backend services in production.
- Strong proficiency in Python, Node.js, or Go.
- Solid experience with PostgreSQL or MongoDB.
- Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud platforms (AWS / GCP).
- Experience with message queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka) is a plus.
- Good written and verbal communication skills.

Nice to have:
- Experience with LLM-based tools or vector databases (Qdrant, Pinecone).
- Contributions to open source projects.

Benefits:
- Competitive salary
- Health insurance
- Flexible work-from-home policy

To apply, send your resume to sanil@ceegees.in with the subject line
"Senior Software Engineer Application".
